Memory Game Rules

  1. Click on any two cards to reveal their symbols.
  2. If the symbols match, they remain face-up.
  3. If the symbols don't match, they flip back face-down.
  4. Remember the positions of the cards as you flip them.
  5. The game continues until all pairs are matched.
  6. Try to match all pairs in the fewest moves possible.
