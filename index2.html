Tic-Tac-Toe Rules

  1. The objective of the game is to be the first player to achieve a straight line (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) of three of their symbols ('X' or 'O') on the grid.
  2. The game ends when one player achieves a straight line of three of their symbols or when all spaces on the grid are filled without a winner (resulting in a draw).
  3. If a player successfully achieves a straight line, they win the game. If all spaces on the grid are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
  4. The first player to win a round earns a point, and a new round begins. The game can be played for multiple rounds to determine the overall winner.
