Hangman Rules

  1. One player thinks of a word and draws dashes representing each letter.
  2. Players guess letters one at a time.
  3. If a guessed letter is in the word, it is revealed; otherwise, a part of the hangman is drawn.
  4. Players win by guessing all the letters before the hangman is fully drawn.
  5. Players lose if the hangman is fully drawn before the word is guessed.
  6. Optionally, players may request a hint for a clue related to the word's category.
